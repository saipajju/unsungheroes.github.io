Unsung Heroes

Matangini Hazra's actions’ impact on present-day India

Being a faithful follower of Gandhi, Matangini’s actions mostly went unnoticed. Unfortunately, historians and architects were unable to note down sufficient information about her actions and the tremendous effect they had during the great Indian struggle for freedom. Matangini Hazra was an extraordinary woman. Herr frail body did not stop her from being an inspiring force in the freedom movement. Even now, she comes out to be one of the most brilliant unsung heroes of India.
