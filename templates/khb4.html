Khudiram Bose was a brave freedom fighter who fought for the independence of our country and laid down his life for it. The revolutionary had a fearless spirit and is known for the attempt to assassinate the district magistrate of Muzaffarpur, Douglas Kingsford.


Khudiram Bose was one of the youngest freedom fighters that gave up their life for the country. Newspaper reports from then have said that he went to the gallows with a smile on his face. Later, Muzaffarpur jail, where he was put to death, was named after him.
